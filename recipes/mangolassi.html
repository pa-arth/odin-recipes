Mango Lassi Recipe

Description

Mango Lassi is a beautiful yogurt drink, both the optimal drink after a hot day of work or just a complement to a classic meal. Mango Lassi includes many probiotic bacteria as well as protein making this delicious drink nutricious as well! While most Mango Lassis are immaculate, you must watch out for posers who water down their drinks and use fake mango concentrate instead of fresh mangos. Keeping this information in mind, Mango Lassi is a drink you're sure to fall in love with at first sip.

Ingredients

  • 3 whole mangos
  • 3 cups yogurt
  • 5 tbsp sugar

Steps

  1. Dice the whole mangos and put them in the blender
  2. Next add the cups of yogurt
  3. Finally, add the tbsp of sugar and blend
  4. Enjoy this simple and tasty beverage
